## 🛑 Slide 2: The Problem
### The Compliance Bottleneck
*   **Manual Rigor**: 100+ page contracts vs. 1,000+ pages of AAOIFI standards.
*   **Human Error**: Fatigue-based oversight leads to non-compliance risks.
*   **Latency**: Sharia approval is often the slowest link in the Murabaha pipeline.
*   **Scalability**: JS Bank cannot scale its Islamic portfolio with linear auditing headcount.

---

## 💡 Slide 3: The Opportunity
### Compliance as a Competitive Edge
*   **Velocity**: Audit turnaround reduced from **hours to seconds**.
*   **Total Coverage**: 100% clause-by-clause evaluation.
*   **Trust**: Deterministic grounding in AAOIFI FAS 28.
*   **Self-Service**: Empowering relationship managers with pre-audit visibility.

---

## ⚙️ Slide 4: The Intelligent Pipeline
### 4-Stage Autonomous Engine
1.  **Ingestion**: High-fidelity PDF extraction and semantic segmentation.
2.  **Retrieval (RAG)**: Pinecone Vector DB matching clauses to specific AAOIFI Standards.
3.  **Governance**: Deterministic **Logic Gates** filter for hard violations.
4.  **Deep Reasoning**: Gemini 1.5 Pro provides multi-clause, nuanced Sharia interpretation.

---

*   **Frontend**: Next.js 15 + Framer Motion (Premium Glassmorphic Design).
